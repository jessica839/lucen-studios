The problems
we solve.

Built from years of managing dive operations, running a five-star resort, directing liveaboard expeditions, and building a dive, snorkel, and tour business from scratch. This is not guesswork. These are familiar problems with practical fixes.

🌊
High season inquiry overload

Peak season hits and your inbox becomes unmanageable. Enquiries come in faster than you can respond. Some get answered. Some don't. Every missed response is a lost booking.

💬
Guest comms that fall through

Pre-arrival information. Waiver reminders. Post-trip follow-up. Review requests. Each one requires someone to remember to send it. When it doesn't happen, your guest experience suffers.

🧠
Everything runs through your best person

Your most experienced guide or instructor carries the whole operation in their head. When they're sick, on holiday, or gone — new staff don't know how to handle things, standards slip, and you're constantly re-explaining. We build systems so that knowledge lives somewhere your whole team can access it.

🔁
Repetitive staff questions

Your instructors and guides spend significant time answering the same guest questions — conditions, equipment, certification requirements, logistics. An AI-powered FAQ handles it automatically, in any language.

📅
Booking and scheduling friction

Availability requests, date changes, group bookings — each one requires back-and-forth. We build automated booking flows and communication sequences that handle the admin so your team handles the guests.

🌐
Multilingual communication gaps

Your guests speak six languages. Your staff speaks two. AI-assisted communication tools mean every guest gets a prompt, professional response in their own language — without adding staff.

The Products

Four systems.
Built for dive shops.

Done for you. We build it, configure it to your operation, and hand it over running. Not a software subscription — a finished system that works from day one.

CREST
Cancellation, Recovery and Empty Seat Text

Empty seats on tomorrow's boat are lost revenue. CREST sends an SMS blast to your local diver database whenever you have last-minute availability — no app, no platform, just a direct message to divers who already know you. Built for markets where divers have local SIM cards and respond fast: Florida, the Caribbean, and similar shore-based local dive communities.

SMS · Local markets · Last-minute fill
GLIDE
Google Listing Impact & Diver Engagement

Your Google rating is the first thing a new diver sees. GLIDE sends an automated review request two hours after every dive or course completes, when the experience is fresh and the diver is still on-site. Simple, triggered, consistent — and because it works off your booking system, it works for every dive shop globally.

Google reviews · Automated · Works globally
TIDE
Trip Itinerary & Diver Experience Automation

The guest communication sequence your team should be running but never has time for. From booking confirmation through pre-dive information, equipment reminders, post-dive follow-up, and a win-back message for divers who haven't returned. Built once, runs automatically, handles every guest the same way whether you're at full capacity or running a single boat.

Full sequence · Booking to win-back · Any volume
REEF
Respond, Engage, Enroll, Follow-up

A WhatsApp AI that handles incoming inquiries around the clock in any language. REEF answers questions about courses, prices, equipment, and availability — qualifies the inquiry, collects contact details, and hands off to your team only when a human is needed. Built for international tourist-facing shops where inquiries arrive in multiple languages at any hour.

WhatsApp AI · 24/7 · Any language
