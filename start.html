Who This Is For

If you're running a small operation
in a state of controlled chaos —

You know the feeling. Busy periods push the inbox past capacity. Your best team member is still answering the same questions they answered last week. Confirmations and follow-ups go out when someone remembers. A new hire has to learn everything from whoever's available to show them.

It works. But it only works because you and your team are constantly plugging the gaps. The business runs on people, not on systems. And when things calm down, you're exhausted — and the same problems are waiting for next time.

That's what we fix. Not with complicated enterprise software. With practical, AI-powered systems that match how your business actually runs.

  • You run a small service business — medical spa, insurance agency, mortgage broker, professional services firm, dive center, surf school, ski school, tour operator, or similar
  • You have between 3 and 30 people on your team, full-time, part-time, or seasonal
  • You handle a significant volume of client inquiries, especially around pricing, availability, and next steps
  • Communication is largely manual — confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups require someone to remember and send them
  • You serve clients who write in multiple languages, or you'd simply like to respond faster and more consistently
  • You know things could run better but the day-to-day never leaves enough time to fix them
What We Build

Concrete systems.
Not strategy documents.

We build the actual systems, test them against your real workflows, and hand them over ready to run. Here's what that looks like in practice.

📬 Inquiry Automation
  • Every inquiry gets a response within minutes — including at 2am when you're asleep
  • AI handles the common questions: pricing, availability, what to expect, next steps
  • Only complex or unusual conversations go to your team
  • Works in multiple languages automatically
📅 Booking Communication
  • Instant booking confirmations sent automatically
  • Pre-visit or pre-appointment reminders with relevant information and next steps
  • Waiver or document reminders timed before the appointment
  • Post-visit review requests and follow-up offers
🎓 Staff Answer System
  • New and seasonal staff can ask questions and get instant, accurate answers
  • How-to guides, procedures, and house rules — all in one place your team can actually access
  • Reduces onboarding time and removes dependence on your busiest person
  • Built from how your business actually runs, not a generic template
🌐 Multilingual Client Communication
  • Client messages answered in their own language, automatically
  • No extra staff required to handle international customers
  • Consistent, professional tone regardless of which language is used
  • Particularly valuable during high-volume inquiry periods
