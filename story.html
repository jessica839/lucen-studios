Our Story

We didn't learn this
in a boardroom.

From a chance meeting on a dive boat in Bali to running a five-star resort in Belize, managing operations across four continents, and building AI systems for small businesses — this is how we got here.

Fifteen years of
real operations.

Most consultants tell you how to run a business. We've actually run them — in a dozen countries, in three languages, under pressure. That's what makes us different. Not our credentials. Our calluses.

2005–2009
Silicon Valley, USA

Jessica starts in software sales.

Four years at the heart of the tech industry learning how technology gets sold, how businesses make buying decisions, and what separates a product that sticks from one that collects dust. A foundation she draws on every day.

2009–2011
Bali · Thailand · Florida Keys

Into the water. Together.

Jessica left Silicon Valley to travel — and met Matthias on a dive boat in Bali. We both ended up in Thailand training as Divemasters, then completed our PADI Instructor certifications in the Florida Keys. The dive industry is unforgiving of poor operations. We learned fast.

2010–2012
Egypt · Thailand · Canada · Honduras

Running operations across four continents.

We instructed in Egypt, managed a dive operation on Koh Phi Phi, led a remote dive lodge in Canada, and earned our IDC Staff Instructor certifications in Honduras — one of the highest qualifications in recreational diving. Every assignment added a different operational context.

2012–2013
Corsica · Thailand

Liveaboard cruise directors.

Multi-day dive expeditions in open water — some of the most logistically complex operations in the industry. Responsible for the full guest experience from departure to return, with no backup and no margin for error.

2013–2014
Mexico · Belize

Married. Then managed a 5-star resort.

We got married in 2013 — then moved to Belize to manage a five-star dive resort. Every touchpoint of the guest experience: first inquiry to final review. Staff, inventory, communications, quality control. At that level, operational failure has a very direct cost. We didn't fail.

"Full cave diving was not what Jessica had in mind for a honeymoon. I maintain it was a perfectly romantic choice. She disagrees. We are still married, which I consider a win." — Matthias
2015–2016
Mexico

Our own dive business.

After years of building systems for other people's operations, we built one for ourselves. Work permit approved, we opened our own dive, snorkel, and tour operation in Mexico — building everything from scratch, the way we'd always known it should be done.

2016–2023
Europe · Zurich · Lucerne

Back to software. With a different perspective.

We moved back to Europe for our daughter's education. Jessica returned to SaaS — bringing field-level operations experience back into software sales and later into marketing. Matthias joined European SaaS companies including PayFit and Beekeeper in Zurich in sales and account management. Both of us watched the same operational problems we'd fixed manually in dive centers repeat themselves in small businesses everywhere.

2026
Lucerne, Switzerland

Gamper Klimmek Consulting.

We founded Gamper Klimmek Consulting to bring everything together. Silicon Valley software sales. Dive operations across four continents. SaaS sales and account management. AI implementation. Two operators who know exactly what it takes to make a small business run better — because we've run them.
