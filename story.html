Our Story

We didn't learn this
in a boardroom.

Most consultants tell you how to run a business. We've actually run them — in a dozen countries, in three languages, under pressure. That's what makes us different. Not our credentials. Our calluses.

2005–09

Silicon Valley, USA

Jessica starts in software sales.

Four years at the heart of the tech industry — learning how technology gets sold, how businesses make buying decisions, and what separates a product that sticks from one that collects dust.

2009–11

Bali · Thailand · Florida Keys

Into the water. Together.

Jessica left Silicon Valley to travel — and met Matthias on a dive boat in Bali. Both completed PADI Instructor certifications in the Florida Keys. The dive industry is unforgiving of poor operations. We learned fast.

2010–12

Egypt · Thailand · Canada · Honduras

Operations across four continents.

We instructed in Egypt, managed a dive operation on Koh Phi Phi, led a remote dive lodge in Canada, and earned IDC Staff Instructor certifications in Honduras. Every assignment added a different operational context.

2013–14

Mexico · Belize

Married. Then managed a 5-star resort.

Married in 2013, then moved to Belize to manage a five-star dive resort — every guest touchpoint from first inquiry to final review. At that level, operational failure has a direct cost. We didn't fail.

2015–16

Mexico

Our own dive business.

After years building systems for other people's operations, we opened our own dive, snorkel, and tour operation in Mexico — from scratch, the way we always knew it should be done.

2016–23

Zurich · Europe

Back to software. With a different perspective.

Matthias joined PayFit and Beekeeper in Zurich in SaaS sales and account management. Jessica returned to software sales, then moved into marketing and communications. Both watched the same operational problems repeat themselves in small businesses everywhere.

2026

Now

Gamper Klimmek Consulting.

We founded Gamper Klimmek Consulting to bring everything together. Silicon Valley software sales. Dive operations across four continents. European SaaS. AI implementation. Two operators who know what it takes to make a small business run better — because we've run them.

"Full cave diving was not what Jessica had in mind for a honeymoon. I maintain it was a perfectly romantic choice. She disagrees. We are still married, which I consider a win."
— Matthias
